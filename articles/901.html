How to Apply Effectively — 7 Tips for 2026

  1. Optimize your resume for ATS with job description keywords.
  2. Build a strong LinkedIn profile with a clear headline.
  3. Customize each cover letter — never copy-paste.
  4. Build a portfolio showcasing your best work.
  5. Practice video interviews (lighting, background, internet).
  6. Apply to 3-5 jobs daily — consistency wins.
  7. Follow up after 5 business days with a professional email.
